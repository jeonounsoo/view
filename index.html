About Us

When Ferdinand Schaller and Tony Weber opened the doors of their modest butcher shop back in 1937,
they were catering to the needs of the German immigrants in their Yorkville neighborhood.
Schaller & Weber was the perfect market for patrons to purchase the cured meats and wursts of their distant homeland.
